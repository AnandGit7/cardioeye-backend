About CardioEye

We believe every heartbeat
tells a story.

CardioEye was built on a simple but powerful idea — that heart disease should be caught early, not discovered late. We combine clinical-grade hardware with intelligent AI to put a cardiologist in your pocket, 24 hours a day.

🎯

Our Mission

To make professional-grade cardiac monitoring accessible to every person on the planet — regardless of geography, income, or access to specialists.

❤️
🔭

Our Vision

A world where cardiovascular disease is the most preventable condition — because everyone can see their heart data in real time and act before it's too late.

⚡

What we stand for

The principles that guide every decision we make.

🔬

Clinical Accuracy First

Every feature we ship is clinically validated. We don't release anything that hasn't been tested in real hospital environments.

🔒

Privacy by Design

Your cardiac data is yours. We are HIPAA-compliant and never sell personal health data. End-to-end encryption, always.

🌍

Accessibility for All

Heart disease doesn't discriminate. We build products for everyone — from metro hospitals to rural clinics in underserved regions.

🤝

Doctor–Patient Partnership

We augment doctors, never replace them. Our tools help physicians make faster, more informed decisions — together.

⚡

Speed Saves Lives

Every second matters in a cardiac event. We obsess over latency, reliability, and real-time delivery in everything we build.

🧠

AI with Accountability

Our AI always explains its recommendations. Explainable, auditable, and always secondary to physician judgment.
